Situation Summary — March 3, 2026
On Feb 28, the US and Israel launched Operation Epic Fury — coordinated airstrikes on Iranian military and nuclear targets. Iran retaliated across Gulf states. The Strait of Hormuz is effectively closed: IRGC blocked all shipping, tanker traffic collapsed from ~50 vessels/day to ~6. Maersk, Hapag-Lloyd, CMA CGM, MSC all suspended transits.

~20M bbl/d of crude + 20% of global LNG normally transits this 21-mile passage. Bypass limited to ~6.5M bbl/d (Saudi Yanbu + UAE Fujairah). Trump says strikes could last 4–5 weeks. Goldman warns Brent could exceed $100/bbl if disruption persists >2 weeks.

84% of Hormuz crude goes to Asia — Japan (90%), S. Korea (73%), India (60%), China (45%). All LNG carriers turned back since Feb 28 21:00 UTC. VLCC rates surged from $45k/day to $200k+/day. EU electricity +28% via gas merit-order. Carbon EUA +8%.
